Escapril prompt: nourishment

Iron lungs and iron maidens.  
Sawblades and pistons.  
Latent power in hard steel.  
Indigestible inertia,  
Grind my guts  
And consume my energy.  
Rebar in concrete,  
Sword in the stone,  
Part the sky and  
Use me as your throne.
